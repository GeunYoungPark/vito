Scent of ViTO

What ViTO offers is Gelato which is healthy and gentle to our bo dies. Its unique texture in the mouth is [simple smoothness]. Upon putting it into mouth, it melts smoothly spreading its aroma and fades away leaving a little aftertaste of sweetness and plain taste.
Beautiful gelato which is made from seasonal natural ingredients that are strictly chosen and made with own recipe based on Italy's authentic way. This is the gelato from ViTO.
You will surely appreciate the taste of ViTO if you try it just once. Its special feature is texture in the mouth. Since we opened, we have been aiming the authentic Itali an gelateria to realize [simple smoothness] every day. It is absolutely ViTO's particular original feeling being different from other gelato that it becomes plain and go through the throat smoothly despite very rich taste at the moment of putting it into your mouth.
What enables it to have such texture in the mouth is using fresh and strictly chosen ingredients such as newly milked fresh milk delivered from a dairy farmer in Aso, Kumamoto. Another factor is that we don't add much sugar, preservatives, control milk fat as half as usual ice cream (3-6%). It is so refreshing that you don't need to drink water later, and it is healthy and gentle to our bodies.
